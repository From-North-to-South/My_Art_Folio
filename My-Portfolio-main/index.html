Learn About Me

5 Years Experience

Hello and welcome! I'm Jeddy Coco, an artist with a passion for creativity that knows no bounds. With over five years of immersive experience in the art world, my journey has been a fusion of diverse styles and techniques, spanning across Manga art, Graffiti art, and the intricate strokes of Calligraphy.
Exploring Artistic Dimensions
My artistic journey began with a deep love for Manga art, where I discovered the power of storytelling through visuals. The dynamic characters, vibrant colors, and captivating narratives fueled my creativity and laid the foundation for my artistic exploration.
Graffiti art became another canvas for me to express myself. The urban landscapes, bold lines, and graffiti's inherent rebellious spirit allowed me to create art that resonates with the energy of the streets, blending messages with visuals in a unique, impactful way.
In the midst of these expressive forms, I found solace in the precision and grace of Calligraphy. The delicate balance of strokes, the poetry within each curve, and the rich cultural history behind this art form deeply influenced my approach to visual storytelling.

Manga Art

Welcome to the world of Manga artistry! Immerse yourself in captivating stories, dynamic characters, and vibrant visuals brought to life all the way from Japan through my Manga art services. As an artist deeply passionate about this genre, I offer a range of services tailored to meet your creative needs.
What I Offer:
1. Character Design and Development: Bringing characters to life is my specialty. Whether you need protagonists, antagonists, or supporting characters, I excel in crafting unique and compelling personalities that resonate with your narrative.
2. Storyboarding and Illustration: From conceptual sketches to detailed storyboards, I'll help visualize your story with expressive and visually engaging illustrations that capture the essence of your narrative.
3. Comic Creation and Sequential Art: Transform your ideas into a visually immersive experience. I specialize in crafting captivating comics and sequential art, guiding the reader through a mesmerizing journey with expertly crafted panels and storytelling techniques.
4. Custom Artwork and Commissions: Looking for a personalized piece or a commissioned artwork? I'm here to turn your ideas into stunning, one-of-a-kind creations that reflect your vision and style.
Why Choose My Manga Art Services?
With years of experience and a deep understanding of the Manga art form, I bring a unique blend of storytelling, creativity, and technical skill to every project. My dedication to capturing emotions, dynamic compositions, and attention to detail ensures that each artwork surpasses expectations.
Let's Create Together!
Embark on an artistic adventure with me. Whether you're an author, game developer, or someone with a story waiting to be told, I'm here to collaborate and bring your vision to life through the captivating world of Manga art.
Thank you for considering my Manga art services. I look forward to collaborating with you and weaving magic through visuals that transcend boundaries and captivate hearts.

Graffiti

Welcome to the vibrant and dynamic world of graffiti art! Dive into the urban landscapes, bold expressions, and visually striking creations offered through my graffiti art services. As an artist passionate about this rebellious and impactful art form, I bring a range of services designed to amplify your creative vision.
What I Offer:
1. Mural Art and Wall Graffiti: Transform blank walls into captivating masterpieces. Whether it's a public mural, commercial space, or personal project, I specialize in creating large-scale graffiti artworks that command attention and infuse life into spaces.
2. Custom Graffiti Lettering and Typography: From stylized fonts to personalized lettering, I craft custom graffiti typography that adds an edgy and expressive touch to branding, logos, merchandise, or any project requiring bold and unique letterforms.
3. Live Event and Workshop Engagements: Bring the energy of graffiti art to your events! I offer live graffiti performances and interactive workshops, allowing audiences to witness the creation process firsthand or even participate in creating collaborative artworks.
4. Urban Art Installations and Collaborations: Let's push boundaries together! I thrive on collaborative projects, including urban art installations, collaborations with fellow artists, or community-driven initiatives that celebrate the spirit of graffiti culture.
Why Choose My Graffiti Art Services?
With years of experience in the graffiti art scene, I blend creativity, urban culture, and a deep understanding of street art to deliver impactful and visually arresting creations. My dedication to exploring new techniques, colors, and styles ensures that each artwork resonates with authenticity and creative energy.
Let's Create Urban Magic!
Join me in celebrating the vibrant and rebellious nature of graffiti art. Whether you're a business owner, event organizer, or enthusiast seeking to infuse spaces with raw energy and visual storytelling, I'm here to collaborate and bring your vision to life through the dynamic world of graffiti art.
Thank you for considering my graffiti art services. I'm excited to collaborate, transform spaces, and create urban art that sparks conversations and inspires communities.
